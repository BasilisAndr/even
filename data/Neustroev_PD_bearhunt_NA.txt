recording equipment and he spoke; when he finished with one topic, BP paused the recording equipment; so there are
different topics in one big file, which were cut into separate sound and video files

Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviation = PDN
\ref Petr Dmitrievich Neustroev was 68 years old at the time of recording, srednee obrazovanie, is trained as a carpenter,
worked as a horse and reindeer herder, is a master in making sleds and lassos; his origins are from Tompo, both parents
from Tompo, but he grew up on some priiskax in the Verxojansk region, and has been living in Sebjan for 40 years -
has nevertheless retained some Tompo dialectal features
