\ref Recorded on 21 March 2008 in the 9th reindeer brigade in their camp (about 1-1.5 hours drive from Sebjan-Küöl) by Brigitte Pakendorf.
Recording was done in one tent – background noises are puppy whining, clothes rubbing against each other, spoons hitting the side of
cups, tea slurping, konfety unwrapping, eating and chavkajut, dogs barking, plane flying overhead, chain saw. BP asked Afanasij
Vasi’levich Krivoshapkin some questions and he answered in Even; cut out the mainly Even parts of the sound file and saved them as
separate files according to topic
\qu Transcribed and translated in Word files by Raisa Petrovna Kuzmina during visit to MPI-EVA in spring 2009; transcribed in ELAN and glossed by BP
in 2010, transcription and translation checked with Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina; Speaker abbreviation = AVK
\ref Speaker is Afanasij Vasi’levich Krivoshapkin, 66 years old at time of recording, "nastavnik" (i.e. mentor) to the younger reindeer herders;
father of Ekaterina Afanas'evna Krivoshapkina
