\ref Recorded on 22 March 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf. This recording took place in the
flat rented by BP and NA in Sebjan-Küöl. Misha was there because he was working as an informant for NA's phonetic
research. After such a session he was asked to tell something in Even and he agreed. Actually NA asked him in a couple
of days before to tell something for the archive. So he seemed to prepare an anecdote (that took about 2 minutes). Then
BP asked him some questions in Even. 
\qu Transcribed by BP, Russian translation by IVK, glossing by BP, all in 2012. Funding by Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant).
Speaker abbreviation = MK
\ref Misha Krivoshapkin was a 17-year old school boy at the time of recording.
