\ref Recorded on 29 June 2009 by Brigitte Pakendorf in Anavgai. The recording was done in the kitchen of RMS's youngest daughter. Only RMS and BP
were actually in the kitchen, while the daughter and Natalia Aralova were in other rooms of the house; in the beginning, they were in the living room
chatting, which was audible in the kitchen, so after RMS had told a fairy tale BP asked them to be quieter and they moved to another room. Overall,
there are hardly any disturbing noises, except that a neighbour started hammering about halfway through. Unfortunately, at the end BP got a coughing
fit that she could not suppress. RMS first told a little fairy tale, and then she recounted some childhood reminiscences. She speaks very softly.

Evgenija Zhivotova????. Glossed by BP in 2016. Funding by the Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant) and CNRS/Université Lyon 2 (laboratoire DDL). Speaker
abbreviation = RMS

librarian. She speaks Russian with her children. She is the sister of Rimma Maksimovna Egorova.
