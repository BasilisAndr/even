\ref Petr Dmitrievich Neustroev was 68 years old at the time of recording, srednee obrazovanie, is trained as a carpenter, worked as a
horse and reindeer herder, is a master in making sleds and lassos; his origins are from Tompo, both parents from Tompo, but he grew
up on some priiskax in the Verxojansk region, and has been living in Sebjan for 40 years - has nevertheless retained some Tompo
dialectal features
