\ref Recorded on 19 Feb 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Brigitte Pakendorf and Natalia Aralova. RDA came to the flat that NA and BP are renting,
where the recording was done in the kitchen. After a cup of tea to warm up we switched on the equipment and she started to narrate
various stories, both about her own life as well as stories that had been told her. It was fairly quiet in the house, though the washbasin
dripped; at a somewhat later stage in the recording the house owner started chopping firewood outside, which might be audible. Since
RDA spoke for one hour, touching upon various topics, we cut up the video file into 7 separate files that deal with separate topics to
facilitate the workflow (transcription, translation, interlinearization); this is the beginning of the recording.
\qu Transcribed (pen and paper) and translated by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina; transcribed in ELAN by BP and glossed by BP. Funding from the
Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS project). Speaker abbreviation = RDA
\ref Raisa Dmitrievna Alekseeva was born 10 Feb 1961, so she was 49 at the time of the recording. She holds a university degree and was
working in the school as psychologist at the time of the recording; every summer, however, she went to the reindeer herd as a "tent
worker" (chumrabotnitsa). She is the sister of Innokentij Dmitrievich Burtsev.
