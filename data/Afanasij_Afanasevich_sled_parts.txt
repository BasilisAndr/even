\ref Recorded on 18 March 2009 in the little winter hut of the 9th reindeer brigade near Sebjan-Küöl (about 1-1.5 hour's drive) by Brigitte
Pakendorf. Afanasij Afanasevič named the parts of a sled while viewing a photo of a sled; unfortunately, the hut filled up as we were
doing this, so there is quite a bit of background noise, a baby crying and gurgling, people talking quite loudly, a truck passing by, fire
crackling
\qu Transcribed by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina and then later translated by her into Russian; transcription in ELAN by BP, glossing by BP. Funding by
Max Planck Society (MPRG on Comparative Population Linguistics). Speaker abbreviation = AAS
\ref Afanasij Afanas'evič Stepanov was 57 years old at the time of recording, used to be a reindeer herder, then glava of the village, owned
one of the shops in the village at the time of recording. Brother of Zoja Afanas'evna Stepanova.
