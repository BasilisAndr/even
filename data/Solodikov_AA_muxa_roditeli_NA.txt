\ref Recorded on 02 July 2009 in Central Kamchatka by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf. This session was made in a
reindeer herd. Since we were sitting in a small yurt, the tripod couldn't be used, so NA was holding the camera and BP was
holding the microphone. There were five people in the yurt: NA, BP, Anatolij Afanasjevich, Gennadij Afanasjevich and a
young herdsman Pavel who flapped in front of camera. This session consists of four recordings: tabun_1, tabun_2,
muxa_roditeli, bearhunt. When we turned off all our devices, Anatolij Afanasjevich told a hunting story about a bear and
agreed to retell it in Even, so it was the last story we recorded with him.
\qu ????Transcribed by NA with Valentina Innokent'evna Axmetova, translated into Russian by VIA, glossed by NA. Funding by the
Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviation = AAS
\ref Anatolij Afanas'evich Solodikov was born in February 1956, so he was 53 at the time of the recording. He finished 8 years of
school and then worked in the reindeer herd as a herder all his life. He was married to Valentina Innokent'evna Axmetova and is
the brother of Gennadij Afanasevich Solodikov. He passed away in July 2016.
