\ref Recorded on 18 March 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf. AVZ came to the flat that NA
and BP were renting in the the early evening specifically to tell a story in Even. It was quiet except for a truck or two
that went by outside. This is a song written by another Sebjan-Küöl resident, Petr Dmitrievich Neustroev. 
\qu Transcribed by Brigitte Pakendorf, transcription checked and Russian translation by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina, glossing by BP.
Funding by the Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviation = AVZ
\ref Aleksandr Vasilevich Zaxarov was born in 1963, i.e. he was 46-47 years old at the time of recording. He has a
college-level education and works as a "metodist" in the "klub". He is married to a Sakha and speaks Sakha and Russian
with her.
