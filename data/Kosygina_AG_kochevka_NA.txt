\ref Recorded on 24 June 2009 in Esso by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf. This short little narrative (only about 4 minutes in length) was recorded in the
living room of the house BP and NA are renting in Esso. AGK came on purpose only for the recording of this text as well as the dictionary of
reindeer-related terms. She had come by the day before, when we discussed what she could talk about, compiling a little outline of all the events that are
important during the yearly cycle of a reindeer herd. At home she had written out what she wanted to say in both Even and Russian, and during the
recording she had read off the Even narrative. Only her, BP and NA were present; the recording was done at noontime.

college, and had worked in the reindeer herd; at the time of the recording she was working as a sanitary worker in the hospital and . Mainly speaks Russian
with husband, even though he is also Even; speaks Russian with her children.
