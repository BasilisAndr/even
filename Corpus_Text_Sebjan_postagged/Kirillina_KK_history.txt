\ref Recorded on 21 March 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Brigitte Pakendorf and Natalia Aralova. KKK came to the flat where NA and BP are staying
on a Sunday morning, after prior arrangement. It turned out that she felt uncomfortable speaking Even to two near-strangers, especially
with a microphone and video camera, so we agreed that she would come again and bring an Even audience with her. However, she did
consent to give a very brief little account of the early history of Sebjan-Küöl, which is this text. It was very quiet during the recording,
which was done in the kitchen of the apartment, and only NA and BP were present.
\qu Transcription, translation and glossing in 2012; transcribed on pen & paper by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina, transcribed in ELAN by BP; Russian
translation by IVK, glossing by BP. Funding by the Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviation = KKK.
\ref Klavdija Konstantinovna Kirillina was 55 years old at the time of recording and a teacher at the school; she also led the after-school group
(kruzhok) on local history and folklore.
