\ref Recorded on 03 March 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf  at the flat rented by NA and BP. The two women were
invited to our place to discuss some Even traditions and customs, and initially we had hoped it would be a conversation between the two of
them. BP & NA were sitting close to them. BP asked a lot of questions, so they were looking at her (though she cannot be seen in the first part
of the recording). For ease of trancription and annotation, the recordings were cut into ten separate files. This one "RDA_TPK_birth" is the
fifth part. 
\qu Transcribed (pen & paper) by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina, transcribed in ELAN by BP, translated into Russian by IVK, glossed by BP. Funding by the
Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviations = RDA, TPK.
\ref Raisa Dmitrievna Alekseeva was born 10 Feb 1961, so she was 49 at the time of the recording. She holds a university degree and was working
in the school as psychologist at the time of the recording; every summer, however, she went to the reindeer herd as a "tent worker"
(chumrabotnitsa). She is the sister of Innokentij Dmitrievich Burtsev.
\ref Tatjana Petrovna has higher education, is a teacher at the school, was 46 years old at times of recording. She is the aunt of Ija Vasil'evna
Krivoshapkina.
