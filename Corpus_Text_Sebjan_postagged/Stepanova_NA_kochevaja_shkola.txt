\ref Recorded on 11 March 2010 in Sebjan-Küöl by Natalia Aralova and Brigitte Pakendorf. This session took place in the flat where BP
and NA lived during their stay in Sebjan-Küöl. NAS was asked to tell something about the nomadic school where she works as a
teacher. Before the recording we talked a little bit about the education and about the school in Sebjan.
\qu Transcribed by Ija Vasil'evna Krivoshapkina (pen and paper), transcribed in ELAN by BP, Russian translation by IVK, glossing by BP.
Funding by the Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant). Speaker abbreviation = NAS.
\ref Nadezhda Alekseevna Stepanova was born in May 1954, so she was 55 at the time of recording. She has higher education and
worked as the teacher of the nomadic branch of the Sebjan school, visiting children of primary school age in the reindeer herding
encampments. She is the sister of Lidija Alekseevna Tulasynova.
