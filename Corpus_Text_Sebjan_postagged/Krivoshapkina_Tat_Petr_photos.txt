me about them in Even; fairly quiet, but fire (zhelezka) can be heard crackling and alarm clock on table ticking away. The photos
from the reindeer herd are presumably at least partly by Piers Vitebsky; the 7th brigade, which was led by Tatjana Petrovna’s
father, is the brigade Vitebsky writes about most in his book; cf. Vitebsky, Piers (2005): The Reindeer People.
