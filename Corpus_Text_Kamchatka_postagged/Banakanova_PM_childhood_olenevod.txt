\ref Recorded on 13 July 2009 in Esso by Brigitte Pakendorf. PMB came to the flat that NA and BP are renting in Esso on a Monday
afternoon; only BP and PMB were present. It was fairly quiet, with all phones and the fridge switched off. The recording was done
in mono with a Marantz recorder; PMB insisted that she did not want to be videotaped, so there is only the audio recording.. We
started off once and broke off, because PMB switched to Russian nearly immediately; the second recording started off somewhat
better, with her concentrating on speaking Even, but she did switch to Russian after about 7 minutes or so, and the overwhelming
majority of the recording is in Russian. Therefore, only the first minutes of Even speech were transcribed and glossed.
\qu In 2016, transcribed by BP, transcription checked and Russian translation by Rimma Maksimovna Egorova, glossing by BP. Funding by the
Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS grant) and CNRS/Universit Lyon 2 (laboratoire DDL). Speaker abbreviation = PMB
\ref Pelageja Mixajlovna Banakanova was born in 1940, so she was 69-70 at the time of the recording. She has six years of schooling
and after that worked in the reindeer herd with her parents.
