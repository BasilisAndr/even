\ref Recorded on 15 July 2009 in Esso by Brigitte Pakendorf. The recording was done in the flat of LGT in the early afternoon, in her room, with
her sitting on the bed. Apart from BP, two of LGT's granddaughters were in the flat, but in a different room. In the beginning, a dog can be
heard yapping outside, and at the end the granddaughters can be heard laughing and talking quite loudly, otherwise it was fairly quiet.  BP
had been told in the beginning to speak up, and so she shouted questions quite loudly in broken Even where possible throughout the
recording. She was sitting on the floor in front of LGT, with the video camera somewhat behind and to the side, so she wasn't able to monitor
it. In the middle of the session, the video camera ran out of tape, so the Marantz was stopped and a new tape was inserted. LGT spoke a lot in
Russian, so that only small sections that were mostly in Even were later on transcribed and glossed.
\qu In 2016, transcribed by BP, transcription checked and Russian translation by Rimma Maksimovna Egorova, glossing by BP. Funding by the Volkswagen
Foundation (DoBeS grant) and CNRS/Universit Lyon 2 (laboratoire DDL). Speaker abbreviation = LGT
\ref Lidija Gavrilevna Tylkanova was born in March 1933, so she was 76 at the time of the recording. She has 5 years of school education and
after that worked as a hunter, living mainly in the forest.
