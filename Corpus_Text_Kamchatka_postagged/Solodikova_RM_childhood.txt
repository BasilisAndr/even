\ref Recorded on 29 June 2009 by Brigitte Pakendorf in Anavgai. The recording was done in the kitchen of RMS's youngest daughter.
Only RMS and BP were actually in the kitchen, while the daughter and Natalia Aralova were in other rooms of the house; in the
beginning, they were in the living room chatting, which was audible in the kitchen, so after RMS had told a fairy tale BP asked
them to be quieter and they moved to another room. Overall, there are hardly any disturbing noises, except that a neighbour
started hammering about halfway through. Unfortunately, at the end BP got a coughing fit that she could not suppress. RMS first
told a little fairy tale, and then she recounted some childhood reminiscences. She speaks very softly.
\qu In 2009, transcribed in ELAN by NA and Rimma Maksimovna Egorova, translated orally into Russian by RME (audiorecordings), Russian
translation entered into ELAN by Evgenija Zhivotova in 2015-2016. Glossed by BP in 2016. Funding by the Volkswagen Foundation (DoBeS
grant) and CNRS/Universit� Lyon 2 (laboratoire DDL). Speaker abbreviation = RMS
\ref Raisa Maksimovna Solodikova was born in 1945, i.e. she was 63-64 at the time of the recording. She had 11 years of schooling and
worked as the school librarian. She speaks Russian with her children. She is the sister of Rimma Maksimovna Egorova.
